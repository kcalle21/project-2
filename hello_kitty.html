Hello Kitty: A Quick Biography

Hello Kitty is a cat girl that was born in London on November 1st,1974. Her parents are Mary White and George White.

She also has a twin sister names Mimmy. Mimmy enjoys patchwork and embroidery. Kitty also has a pet Persian cat named Charmmy Kitty.

She also has a pet hamster named Sugar, who was a gift from her friend Daniel.

Hello Kitty is very energetic, bright, and friendly. Her hobbies include: traveling, music, and eating cookies Mimmy and her mom make.
