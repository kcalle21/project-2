Snoopy- One of my Favorite Characters

Snoopy is a character from the comic "The Peanuts". He is the main character's, Charlie Brown, dog.

He is known for his silly and goofy personality as well as his many little jokes he pulls on Charlie and his many adventures.

Snoopy's Inspiration came from the cartoonist's dog Spike, the dog's antics inspired Schul'z imagination.

He is a beagle but not any average beagle, he love not only reading books but also writing them.

He is also a collector of fine art and loves root beer. His imagination makes sure his life is never boring.

As the "Flying Ace" he battles the "Red Baron" and as "Joe Cool" he is the big dog on Campus.

Snoopy's best friend is Woodstock a little yellow bird. However he is not a great flyer, all he knows is that him and snoopy are great pals.

Snoopy is the only one that can understand Woodstock's little chirps.
